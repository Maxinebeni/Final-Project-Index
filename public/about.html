About Health Clique

About Us

Health Clique connects healthcare professionals and communities in Zimbabwe and Rwanda, closing critical knowledge gaps and driving better healthcare outcomes for all. With a keen focus on bridging knowledge gaps prevalent in the health sectors of both nations, we strive to enhance healthcare outcomes through transformative digital interactions. Join the conversation, share expertise, and solve challenges together - one click at a time.

Our Mission

To empower healthcare communities fostering collaboration, knowledge exchange, and collective problem-solving to bridge healthcare gaps and significantly improve healthcare outcomes for all.

Our Vision

Our vision is to seamlessly connect medical professionals, communities, and stakeholders across the continent, fostering a vibrant ecosystem of knowledge exchange, collaboration, and innovation.
